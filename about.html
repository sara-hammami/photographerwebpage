who am I?

Well, let's start with the basics: I'm rnveer, I'm in my early 37s, I'm a photographer. I love all forms of art, I call myself a “chronic creative”, it seems like every few month I pick up a new medium of art to learn.One of my favorite parts of my job is you: the people I get to meet and work with. It is such an honor to be asked to capture important moments in people’s lives. To be asked to be a part of important events, hearing stories, witnessing love stories unfold, watching kids grow up, it is such a gift to call this my job!
